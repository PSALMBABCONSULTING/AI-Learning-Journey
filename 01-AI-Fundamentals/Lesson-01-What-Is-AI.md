# Lesson 1 – What Is Artificial Intelligence?

## Definition

Artificial Intelligence is the use of computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

## AI in Healthcare Analytics

AI can support healthcare by helping with:

- Prediction
- Pattern detection
- Automation
- Summarization
- Decision support

## Traditional Analytics vs AI

Traditional analytics explains what happened.

Machine Learning predicts what is likely to happen.

Generative AI creates, summarizes, explains, and assists.

## My Reflection

As a healthcare analyst, I can use AI to move beyond reporting into prediction, automation, and strategic decision support.
